Introduction.

Welcome to Jointly, a team-based platform for conducting group exercises! Today, we will play different games to study the emergence and change of collective identity in groups and teams. In this series of exercises, you will be teamed with other players. These are your teammates. You will have the opportunity to collaborate with your teammates to earn more of a reward. In each of the games, you will have the opportunity to earn a non-trivial amount of money based on how well your team performs.
