Here's how it works:

1. You will receive assigned letters that appear in BLUE.

2. Form a valid English word that is 3 letters or longer and enter it into the submission box. For example, the letters R,O,L can be used to spell the word “ROLL” (case does not matter: roll = ROLL = roLL, etc.).

3. The word you submit will appear with all the other words that the team submits. They are all counted together. The current record shows the best score so far. This is what you want to beat, and maximize your earnings. You can only submit the same word once. However, you can submit the same word that another player submitted. For example, if Player 1 submits “roll”, Player 2 can also submit “roll”.

4. To get more letters to make more words, ask for help from your team! We have made it possible for you and your team to copy each others’ letters. You have been randomly assigned to view some of your teammates’ letters. Click on the button to request more letters from your team, and then click on a letter that you would like. The letter will appear ORANGE in your team letters box, and a request will be sent to your teammate. When your teammate copies the letter, it will turn GREEN and be available for use.

5. Your teammates can also request letter copies from you. In order for other players to receive a copy of your letter, you must approve them by clicking on the button that appears. Share with your teammates by answering their requests by clicking the BLUE buttons.
