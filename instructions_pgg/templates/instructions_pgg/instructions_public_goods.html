Here's how it works:

1. You and every member of your team will decide how much of your earnings to contribute to a team account. This amount will be doubled, and then split evenly for everyone on your team, regardless of their contribution amount. No one on your team will know how much you contribute. Earnings that are not sent to the team account will be kept in your individual account. These will not be doubled. Your total earnings will be the sum of your team award amount and your individual account.


2. You will have {{session.config.pgg_timeout_min}} minute(s) to make a decision. How much you choose to invest is completely up to you. The less you contribute, the more you keep and benefit from others' contributions. However, if everyone on the team contributes less, then everyone will receive less. If you do not contribute before the time runs out, you will not be eligible to receive the earnings.
