Just for participating, we will now give each member of your team an additional {{session.config.pgg_bonus|c}}. But, we will also give your team the chance to double their earnings.

Here's how it works:

1. On the next page, your additional earnings appear in a box like the one shown below. You can keep this amount if you choose to contribute nothing to the group.

2. If you want to contribute, enter in the amount of your additional earnings that you would like to contribute to the group. We will take all the contributions from the group and double the amount. It will then be redistributed to the team equally. If everyone on the team contributes their full amount, then the amount of reward given to all the players will be doubled.

3. You will have {{session.config.pgg_timeout_min}} minute(s) to make a decision. Having a hard time deciding how much to contribute? No problem. We will provide a table that shows how much you can expect to earn based on how much you contribute and the average contribution put in by members of your team. You always end up with a larger bonus by letting everyone else contribute except you; however, bear in mind that if no one contributes to the pool because each person expects everyone else to, then everyone will miss the opportunity to double their winnings.
